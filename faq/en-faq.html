There are some methods with optional parameters. If I leave these parameters empty, such methods performs with errors. What should I do to execute its correctly?
  • If you wouldn't to use optional parameter, you should remove it from parameter tree.
  • Primitive data types parameters are removed by clicking at left cross, composite parameters by clicking at right cross.
Will be there XML format support?
  • Yes.
  • We are working on this issue now.
Can I recall method after its calling with saving input parameters in current state?
  • Yes.
  • You just need to click "Call" button once more.
Where can I look at definition of input and output method parameters?
  • Firstly you need to choose desired method from Allow method section at left side.
  • Then click at plus on the left from method title and pick input or output parameters.
May I restore parameters, If I've removed its from parameters tree.
  • No, you may not.
  • You should to reopen corresponding method.
